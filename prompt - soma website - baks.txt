
Prompt: Dr. Soma Mandal MD Authority Hub

1. Project Overview

Intent: Rebuild somamandalmd.com to transition from a clinical physician site to a "Medical Authority & Media Hub." The site must bridge the gap between Dr. Mandal’s roles as an NYU and Oxford-trained physician and a leading voice for midlife women on YouTube and social media. 

2. Visual & Brand Aesthetic

-- ** Read and familiarize yourself with the Soma Mandal brand guidelines **
- **Identity:** A bridge between a biological research lab and an avant-garde luxury magazine.
-- Persona: "The Warm Expert." (Sophisticated, clinical, yet highly accessible).
-- Palette: Muted neutrals, soft sage, and slate blues to evoke calm and professional trust. Blend this with her signature pink color from the hero; use that pink color as the accent color. Background could be a gradient of the accent and sage colors so that it all blends well.
-- Typography: Elegant serif for headers (authority) paired with clean sans-serif for body text (readability).
-- Key Design Elements: High-use of white space, rounded corners, and "cards" to organize multimedia content.

3. Core Architecture (Sitemap)

-- Home: A "Story-Led" hero section highlighting her 3 pillars: Physician, Author, Speaker.

-- Soma Says (Podcast/Video Hub): A dedicated, searchable gallery of her YouTube content.

-- The Books: A high-conversion landing page for Menopause I Do Not Fear You with integrated "Buy Now" and "Review" sections. So the same for her second book "Midlife Masala"

-- Speaker/Media: A professional "One-Sheet" style page for event planners.
-- Clinical Practice: Streamlined section with a link to her HMH practice page.

4. Technical Requirements & Integrations
-- YouTube Embedding: Create a dynamic "Featured Video" section on the homepage that pulls the latest upload from @SomaSays.

-- Video Gallery: Build a category-based video wall (e.g., Menopause, Longevity, Mental Health) using embedded YouTube players that maintain site speed (lazy-loading).

-- Conversion Optimization:
	-- Sticky "Book Dr. Mandal" button for speaking.
-- Newsletter "Lead Magnet" (e.g., "The Midlife Blueprint PDF").
-- Mobile-First: Ensure the video gallery is highly responsive and easy to navigate via thumb-taps.

5. Implementation Instructions for the AI

-- "Make sure this website can easily be installed on Squarespace"
-- "Focus on a single-page scrolling architecture for the homepage to maximize narrative flow."
-- "Use the Youtube video information you've gathered, and present it in a tasteful, usable way"



### Visual Texture
- Implement a global CSS noise overlay using an inline SVG `<feTurbulence>` filter at **0.05 opacity** to eliminate flat digital gradients.
- Use a `rounded-[2rem]` to `rounded-[3rem]` radius system for all containers. No sharp corners anywhere.

### Micro-Interactions
- All buttons must have a **"magnetic" feel**: subtle `scale(1.03)` on hover with `cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94)`.
- Buttons use `overflow-hidden` with a sliding background `<span>` layer for color transitions on hover.
- Links and interactive elements get a `translateY(-1px)` lift on hover.

### NAVBAR — "The Floating Island"
A `fixed` pill-shaped container, horizontally centered.
- **Morphing Logic:** Transparent with light text at hero top. Transitions to `bg-[background]/60 backdrop-blur-xl` with primary-colored text and a subtle `border` when scrolled past the hero. Use `IntersectionObserver` or ScrollTrigger.
- Contains: Logo (brand name as text), 3-4 nav links, CTA button (accent color).

- **No placeholders.** Every card, every label, every animation must be fully implemented and functional.
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first. Stack cards vertically on mobile. Reduce hero font sizes. Collapse navbar into a minimal version.
